Digital Productivity

Digital tools can boost output by reducing manual steps and improving coordination.

Examples

  • Collaboration suites like Google Workspace and Microsoft 365.
  • Project tools such as Trello or Asana to track tasks.
  • Automation & AI that summarise, draft and analyse faster.

Balanced View

  • Speed and quality improve, but training and focus are needed.
  • Equity matters: ensure device and internet access for everyone.
