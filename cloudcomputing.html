Cloud Computing

Rent computing power, storage and software over the internet instead of buying hardware.

Key Benefits

  • Scalability: add or reduce capacity in minutes.
  • Lower upfront cost: pay‑as‑you‑go replaces big purchases.
  • Access anywhere: learn and work from school, home or overseas.

Issues and Risks

  • Security & privacy: protect data with encryption, backups and access control.
  • Dependence: outages or vendor lock‑in can pause work.
  • Ongoing spend: poor management can increase monthly bills.

Explore providers: AWS, Azure, Google Cloud.
